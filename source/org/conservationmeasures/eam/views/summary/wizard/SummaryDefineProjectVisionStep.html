1B. Clear & Common Purpose:

Define a Vision Statement

Add your project's vision statement vision statement to the appropriate fields.

The full version should contain the complete description of your project’s vision. The short version should be a 3-5 word summary of the vision.

Review the criteria for a good vision statement and revise your statement as necessary.

 

A vision statement is a general summary of the desired state or ultimate condition of the project area that a project is working to achieve. A good vision statement meets the criteria of being:

  • Relatively General - Broadly defined to encompass a broad range of potential project activities

  • Visionary - Inspirational in outlining the desired change in the state of the targets toward which the project is working

  • Brief - Simple and succinct so that all project participants can remember it
