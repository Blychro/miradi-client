Intro to Work Plan View

This view enables you to develop a work plan for your project. A work plan shows the specific activities / methods and tasks that you need to undertake to implement your strategic and monitoring plans. As a general rule, you should have a detailed work plan for the coming 2 to 4 quarters and a rough plan for the years after that.

 

Navigation Hints for the Work Plan View

Click on the Work Plan tab to see an overview of your plan. In the Work Plan tab, clicking on the + and - symbols enables you to expand or collapse different parts of the hierarchy. Items in the table can include activities, methods, and tasks (note that tasks apply to both activities and methods). Click on the buttons on the right to add, delete, or move an item up and down the hierarchy.

The bottom panel is used to assign resources to each item. Assignments can be made at the level of an activity/method or its component tasks.

You can edit the name of activity or method directly on the work plan tab or by clicking on the Activities or Methods tabs. Clicking on a row in the table will allow you to bring up and edit the information for that row. Click on the Resources tab to create, edit, or delete a resource.
