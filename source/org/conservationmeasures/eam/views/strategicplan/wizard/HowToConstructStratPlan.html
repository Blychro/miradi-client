Strategic Plan View

This view enables you to develop a strategic plan for your project. A strategic plan shows the relationship between your project's goals, objectives, and strategies.

Click next to begin developing a strategic plan for your project.



 


Navigation Hints for the Strategic Plan View

Click on the strategic plan tab to see an overview of your plan.

= Goal
= Objective
= Strategy

In the strategic plan tab, clicking on the + and - symbols enables you to expand or collapse different parts of the hierarchy. You can also record estimates of the cost and dates for each item (these column will eventually be derived from other views).

Click on the other tabs to edit your goals, objectives, and strategies. Use the create button to add a new row, the modify button to edit a selected row, and the delete button to remove a selected row. Many of the columns (e.g. factors, direct threats) are automatically generated from the diagram.
