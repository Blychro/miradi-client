Intro to Strategic Plan View

This view enables you to develop a strategic plan for your project. A strategic plan shows the relationship between your project's goals, objectives, and strategies.

Click next to begin developing a strategic plan for your project.



 


Navigation Hints for the Strategic Plan View

Click on the strategic plan tab to see an overview of your plan.Key symbols include:

= Goal
= Objective
= Strategy

In the strategic plan tab, clicking on the + and - symbols enables you to expand or collapse different parts of the hierarchy.

Click on the other tabs to edit your goals, objectives, and strategies. Clicking on a row in the table will allow you to bring up and edit the information for that row. Much of this information is automatically generated from the diagram. To create or delete a goal, objective, or strategy, you have to go the diagram and use the properties dialogue for the relevant factor.
