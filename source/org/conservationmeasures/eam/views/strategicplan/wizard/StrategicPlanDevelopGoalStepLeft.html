2A. Develop Clear Goals and Objectives

Develop Goals

Goals are linked to targets. If you used the detailed method of viability analysis, you have already created goals for your targets, and you can skip ahead to objectives. If you used the basic method for any targets, you should create your goals now.

To create a new goal, double click on a target and then click on the <goals> tab in the properties dialogue box. Click on the <create goal> button at the side of the page. Fill in the following fields:

  • Short Label: The text that will appear in the goal symbol on the diagram. Often a letter (e.g., "A" or "D").

  • Goal: The title of the goal. Generally 3-5 words (e.g., Maintain Seabird Populations)

  • Full Text: The complete goal statement.



To edit or delete a goal, click on the desired row and then either edit the text in the box at the bottom or click on the <delete goal> button.
