Help with Miradi Software

Miradi Version 1.0 Beta is being released on a limited basis for testing and feedback. As such, we have not yet developed the help system that we will deploy when we roll-out the full version.  That said, in the interim, we will be compiling a list of answers to frequently asked questions at:

www.help.Miradi.org

Also, if you have questions or feedback, please send comments to:

support@miradi.org

feedback@miradi.org 

We will endeavor to answer your questions as soon as possible.

Finally, we will be establishing contact points within each of the major CMP organzations who have experience with Miradi Software and may be able to help you with questions that you have.
