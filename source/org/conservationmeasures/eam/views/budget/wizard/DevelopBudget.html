Financial View

This view currently enables you to develop a budget for your project (in the future, it will also enable you to manage actual expenditures). A budget shows the costs and funding sources for the activities/methods and tasks that you need to undertake to implement your strategic and monitoring plans for each quarter.

Hint:The budget view takes the same table that you developed in your work plan and adds a financial dimension to it.

Click next to begin developing a budget for your project.

Navigation Hints for the Budget View

Click on the Budget tab to see an overview of your budget. In the Budget tab, clicking on the + and - symbols enables you to expand or collapse different parts of the hierarchy. Items in the table can include activities, methods, and tasks (note that tasks apply to both activities and methods). Click on the buttons on the right to add, delete, or move an item up and down the hierarchy (note that this is the same functionality as in the Work Plan).

The bottom panel is used to assign resources to each item and to estimate the costs of these resources. Assignments can be made at the level of an activity/method or its component tasks. Each assignment can specify an accounting code and a funding source.

You can edit accounting codes and funding sources directly by clicking on the relevant tabs. Clicking on a row in the table will allow you to bring up and edit the information for that row. Click on the Resources tab to create, edit, or delete a resource.
