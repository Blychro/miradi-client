Check Overall Threat Rating

Once you have completed your ratings for all three criteria, the software automatically calculates the overall threat rating for this target and threat combination. You should check whether you agree with this overall rating. If it does not make sense, go back and adjust the ratings for the specific criteria.



Overall Threat Rating -- Miradi calculates threat ratings using a rule-based system for combining the scope, severity, and irreversibility criteria.
