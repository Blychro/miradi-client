Intro to Threat Rating View

The table below contains a summary of your threat ratings. Threat/Target combinations that have been rated should appear colored in.

If there is a threat or target that is missing, return to the diagram view to add it. If a threat is not linked to a specific target, you can create this by clicking on a blank cell in the table (note that this also creates a corresponding link in the diagram).

Click next to continue with the threat rating process.



Guide to the Threat Rating Summary Table

The far left hand column is a listing of all threats and the top row is a listing of all targets for your project. To sort the table, click on any cell in the header row to sort by that column. You can also move or change the width of columns.

The cells of the table show the overall threat ratings for each threat on each target that it affects. You can click on a cell to bring up the specific criteria that go into the overall threat ratings. To create a new link between a threat and target, click on a blank cell in the table.

Finally, the table contains three types of summary ratings:

  • The column on the far right contains a summary of the overall rating of each threat across all targets.

  • The row on the bottom contains a summary of the overall threat rating for each target.

  • Finally, the lowest right hand cell contains a summary of the threat rating for your entire project.

These summary ratings are not entered directly, but are calculated as you rate the specific effects of threats on targets in the cells of the table.
