1C. Understand Project Context:

Link Your Direct Threats to Targets

Many threats affect more than one target. To link your threat to another target, select the direct threat, hold the <Shift> key and then select the desired target. Then click on the <Add a Link> button (the arrow).

You can also link direct threats to each other using the <Add a Link> button.

 

Hint: To keep your model reasonably simple and to make it an effective communication tool, it is suggested to show the major relationships between direct threats and targets. If you have a direct threat that has a negligible impact on a target, you should consider not including that relationship in your model.

Remember, building your model is as much an art as a science. It will not be perfect, but it should do a reasonable job of representing what is happening at your site.
