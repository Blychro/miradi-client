1D. Analyze Project Situation:

Identify Indirect Threats and Opportunities

To insert contributing factor and opportunities, select a threat and brainstorm what you think is driving that threat. Select the threat and click the <Insert Contributing Factor> button on the legend or toolbar.

The software will add a new contributing factor and open a dialog box in which you can type the name and a comment. Using the mouse or arrow keys, move the factor to wherever you want it to be. In most models, factors further away from the target are placed out to the left-hand side of the page.
