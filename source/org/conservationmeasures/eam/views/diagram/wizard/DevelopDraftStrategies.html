2B. Develop Strategies

Identify Key Factors: Develop Draft Strategies

Once you have isolated the chain that you want to work on, brainstorm draft strategies that your team could use at various points along the chain. Select the factor and click the <Insert a Draft Strategy> button on the toolbar or legend.

The software will add a new draft strategy linked to the target and open a dialogue box. Type a name for the strategy and a comment if necessary.



 


A strategy describes a group of actions with a common focus that work together to reduce threats, capitalize on opportunities, or restore natural systems. A good strategy meets the criteria of being:

  • Linked - Directly affects one or more critical factors

  • Focused - Outlines specific courses of action to be carried out

  • Feasible - Accomplishable in light of the project's resources and constraints

  • Appropriate - Acceptable to and falling within site-specific cultural, social, and biological norms

A draft strategy is one that you have brainstormed, but not yet decided to implement.
