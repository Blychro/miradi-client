2B. Develop Strategies

Rank Draft Strategies

Once you have developed your draft strategies, you rate each one in terms of its impact, duration, feasibility, and cost by double clicking on a draft strategy and using the drop-down ratings in the dialog box.

Once you have developed all your draft strategies, you can decide which one(s) you want to carry forward. Unchecking <draft> in the status field in the dialogue box will convert the strategy from a light yellow "draft strategy" to a dark yellow "strategy."

Click the <Show Full Model> button (available on either the toolbar or by right-clicking the mouse) to go back to the full model and hide all your draft strategies. Note that your draft strategies are still retained in <Show Only Selected Chain> mode.



Draft strategies can be rated on the following criteria:

  • Impact - Will the strategy have a high or low level of impact on the situation at your project site?

  • Duration - Will the strategy lead to permanent or long-term change or will it only have short-term impact?

  • Feasibility - Is the strategy easy or extremely difficult to carry out?

  • Cost - Is the strategy inexpensive to carry out? Or is it prohibitively expensive?

These ratings are then rolled-up to give an overall summary rating for the strategy. Note that if a draft strategy is assigned any "red" colored rating, then the summary rating will also be "red."
